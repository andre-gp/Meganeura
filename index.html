Check it Out

Farm Your Friends is a fast-paced couch competitive farming game for 4 players. This farming life isn't as calm and peaceful as you'd expect: to become the best farmer you will need unusual methods to prevent your enemies from succeding.

The matches lasts 5 minutes, making it a frenetic and chaotic experience. Try selling as much as you can to be the winner while facing other players. You have to find the balance between taking care of your farm and sabotaging the other's - but you won't have time to think it through.

About Us

We are five independent game developers with a dream of making amazing games. We've met during game design college in Belo Horizonte and since then we've been working together as an indie studio called Meganeura Games.
